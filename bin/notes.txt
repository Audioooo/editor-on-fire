Note controls:
   Change note length:  Scroll wheel or [ and ]
   Toggle lanes: SHIFT+#
   Star power: CTRL+W  Tremolo: CTRL+SHIFT+O
   Crazy: T   HO: H   PO: P  Slider: SHIFT+S
Drum controls:
   Mark drum roll: CTRL+SHIFT+O
   Toggle Y/B/G cymbal: CTRL+Y/B/G
   Toggle Expert+ bass: CTRL+E
   Toggle Y Open/Pedal/Sizzle hi hat: SHIFT+O/P/S
   Toggle R rim shot: SHIFT+R
Lyric controls:
   Edit: L    Split: SHIFT+S  Freestyle: F
   Mark lyric line: CTRL+M or CTRL+X
Pro guitar controls:
   Set fret # for selected gems:  CTRL+#, CTRL+F#
   Edit note:  N    Edit fingering: F
   Tap: CTRL+T    Bend: CTRL+B     P. mute: CTRL+M
   Harm.: CTRL+H  Vibrato: SHIFT+V  Ghost: CTRL+G
   S. mute: SHIFT+X        Unpitch. slide:  CTRL+U
   Pop: CTRL+SHIFT+P      Slap: CTRL+SHIFT+S
   Accent: CTRL+SHIFT+A  P. Harm: SHIFT+H
   Ignore: CTRL+SHIFT+I    Linknext: SHIFT+N
